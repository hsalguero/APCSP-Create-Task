Helmer and Miguel's Really Cool Project


Your friend was acting suspicious and somebody alerted the guards. You hear over the intercom that a lockdown is taking place. The security guards spend 2 hours searching for your friend. You had to hold in your urge to go to the bathroom and now you must wait throughout traffic until you can relieve yourself.


This game takes you through the rigorous day-to-day occurences of the UCVTS school district. From culinary fire drills in UCTECH to 2-hour-long lockdowns, this game is sure to knock your socks off and give you the full unfiltered UCVTS experience!
