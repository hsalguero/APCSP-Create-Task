The Freshman Experience


You barely make it to the bus as its pulling off and have to hang off the emergency exit door. Your grip loosens and you are caught by Mr. Wilson, who was driving his sweet new convertible a little too close to the back of the bus. He kindly offers to take you home in exchange for a 512 GB SSD you were luckily carrying in your back pocket


This game takes you through the rigorous day-to-day occurences of the UCVTS school district. From culinary fire drills in UCTECH to 2-hour-long lockdowns, this game is sure to knock your socks off and give you the full unfiltered UCVTS experience!
