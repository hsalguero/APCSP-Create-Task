Helmer and Miguel's Really Cool Project


You dance your way out of the situation. The dance teacher sees your skills and lands you a role in a blockbuster musical. You have the leading role and everybody is so impressed that you are given more roles and become famous. How will you spend your retirement?


This game takes you through the rigorous day-to-day occurences of the UCVTS school district. From culinary fire drills in UCTECH to 2-hour-long lockdowns, this game is sure to knock your socks off and give you the full unfiltered UCVTS experience!
